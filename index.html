About Us

Keys Western Music Is The Best Place To Kick Start your Music

For Students who want to learn Keyboard, Guitar, Flute, Sing and Master Western Music.
While in the UK Hema was a radio jockey for BBC Radio Manchester. Her programme, Eastwards Northwestwards, was the programme for the local Asian community. While earning her ABRSM qualifications for the piano, the flute, the guitar and singing, she was the lead vocalist and guitarist for the beat group Golden Eagles.

After shifting to Hyderabad in 1989,she started Keys Western Music,the first of its kind in Hyderabad to teach modern western music.
Hema uses the Suzuki method.
Training is one -to- one rather than mass classes.
Music Composition is undertaken for advertisements and events.
Orchestra (Western or Bollywood) is available for Parties, Banquets and Conferences.

Certified and Award Winning Teachers

For 15 years, our music school has been a beacon of excellence, largely due to our exceptional teachers. Each of our instructors is a highly skilled professional with extensive experience in their respective fields. They bring a wealth of knowledge, a passion for music, and a dedication to teaching that inspires students of all ages and skill levels. Our teachers hold advanced degrees from prestigious music institutions and have performed on national and international stages. They stay current with the latest teaching methods and continually refine their skills to provide the best possible instruction. This commitment to excellence ensures that our students receive a high-quality education that is both comprehensive and engaging.
